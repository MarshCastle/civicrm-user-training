# Course outline

## Aim

Give you the skills that will allow you to use and administer CiviCRM.

## Objectives

- Understand how the CiviCRM community works and where you can find further information and support
- Know how to use CiviCRM as a day to day user
- Be able to plan and configure CiviCRM installations
- Understand the different CiviCRM components and how they interact with each other
- Work through practical examples and exercises related to the course material
